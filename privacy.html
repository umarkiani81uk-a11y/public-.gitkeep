Booking Tip Terms

Privacy

This website is a simple booking request page. It does not store customer details in a database. Booking requests are sent to the driver via your chosen messaging method (e.g., WhatsApp).

What you may share

  • Name and phone number
  • Pickup and destination
  • Date/time of travel
  • Passengers/luggage details
  • Flight number (if airport pickup)

How it’s used

  • To provide a quote and confirm a booking
  • To contact you about pickup timing, changes, or issues
  • To issue receipts if requested

Third-party services

  • Tips are processed by the payment provider linked on the tip page.
  • Messaging apps (WhatsApp/SMS) are controlled by their own privacy policies.

Contact

If you want your message history deleted, contact the business directly.

Tip: If you later add an online form that emails you, you may want a more detailed GDPR privacy notice.
